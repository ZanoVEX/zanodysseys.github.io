Northern Tanzania

The legendary safari heart of Tanzania — endless plains, ancient craters, and iconic wildlife encounters.

The Northern Safari Circuit

Northern Tanzania is home to Africa’s most famous safari destinations. This region offers classic wildlife experiences, dramatic landscapes, and some of the highest concentrations of animals on the continent.

From vast savannahs to volcanic craters, each park tells a different story of nature, movement, and survival.

Serengeti

Vast plains and the Great Migration

Serengeti National Park

Northern Tanzania

The Serengeti is one of the world’s most iconic safari destinations. Famous for the Great Migration, it is home to millions of wildebeest, zebras, predators, and a year-round abundance of wildlife.

  • The Great Wildebeest Migration
  • Big Five safari experiences
  • Endless open savannah landscapes
  • Exceptional predator sightings
  • Classic African safari atmosphere

Ngorongoro

A natural wonder carved by ancient volcanoes

Ngorongoro Crater

UNESCO World Heritage Site

The Ngorongoro Crater is the largest intact volcanic caldera on Earth. Its enclosed ecosystem supports an extraordinary density of wildlife, making it one of the best places in Africa to see animals in a single day.

  • High concentration of wildlife
  • Excellent chance to see the Big Five
  • Dramatic crater landscapes
  • Year-round game viewing
  • Cultural encounters with Maasai communities

Tarangire

Elephants, baobabs, and quiet wilderness

Tarangire National Park

Northern Tanzania

Tarangire is known for its giant baobab trees and large elephant herds. Less crowded than other northern parks, it offers a peaceful safari experience with beautiful scenery and seasonal wildlife movement.

  • Large elephant populations
  • Iconic baobab landscapes
  • Excellent birdlife
  • Quiet and uncrowded safari routes
  • Ideal for photography and nature lovers
