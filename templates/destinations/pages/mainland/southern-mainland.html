Southern Tanzania

Remote wilderness, vast landscapes, and untamed safari experiences — where nature still rules.

The Southern Safari Circuit

Southern Tanzania offers a raw and authentic safari experience. This region is defined by immense protected areas, fewer visitors, and a deep sense of isolation that allows wildlife to thrive.

It is ideal for travelers seeking space, silence, and true connection with untamed African landscapes.

Ruaha

Rugged landscapes and powerful wildlife encounters

Ruaha National Park

Southern Tanzania

Ruaha is Tanzania’s largest national park and one of its most dramatic. Characterized by rolling hills, ancient baobabs, and the Great Ruaha River, it is home to large predators and diverse ecosystems.

  • Large populations of lions and elephants
  • Scenic river systems and rugged terrain
  • Excellent predator sightings
  • Less crowded, exclusive safari experience
  • Ideal for experienced safari travelers

Mikumi

Open plains and accessible safari adventures

Mikumi National Park

Southern Tanzania

Mikumi is one of Tanzania’s most accessible national parks, known for its wide open floodplains and scenic mountain backdrop. It offers excellent wildlife viewing with minimal travel time from Dar es Salaam.

  • Easy access from Dar es Salaam
  • Open savannah landscapes
  • Good variety of wildlife
  • Ideal for short safaris
  • Perfect for first-time safari travelers
