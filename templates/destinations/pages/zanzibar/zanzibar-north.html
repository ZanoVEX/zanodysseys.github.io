North Zanzibar

Home to Zanzibar’s most famous beaches — where sunsets, soft sands, and ocean life define island freedom.

Nungwi & Kendwa Coastline

The northern tip of Zanzibar offers year-round swimming, vibrant beach life, and some of the island’s most iconic sunsets.

Each area has its own character — from lively evenings to peaceful barefoot days.

Nungwi

Energy, culture, and unforgettable island sunsets

Nungwi Beach

North-West Zanzibar

Nungwi is one of Zanzibar’s most vibrant coastal towns. Known for its lively atmosphere, dhow-building heritage, and beachside sunsets, it blends local life with international energy.

  • Calm swimming beaches year-round
  • Sunset dhow cruises
  • Beach bars & nightlife
  • Fishing village culture
  • Easy access to excursions

Kendwa

White sands, calm waters, and relaxed island rhythm

Kendwa Beach

North Zanzibar Coast

Kendwa is known for its wide white-sand beaches and tranquil atmosphere. Perfect for travelers seeking relaxation, romance, and uninterrupted ocean views.

  • Wide beaches with soft sand
  • Excellent swimming conditions
  • Peaceful sunsets
  • Ideal for couples & families
  • Famous full-moon beach nights
